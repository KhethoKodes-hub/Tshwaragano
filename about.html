Who We Are

Tshwaragano Entle is a 100% black-owned company established to provide quality and reliable services to the local community. Founded and managed by Miss Annah Nangura Greda Wanjamba, our company specializes in construction, cleaning, catering, and supplies. We are committed to making positive contributions to our community while eradicating social challenges and promoting financial independence.

Our name "Tshwaragano" embodies unity and collaboration, which reflects our approach to business. We pride ourselves on understanding the individual needs of each customer and delivering ongoing quality and reliable service based on trust. As a company, we recognize the need for our customers to project a professional and environmentally sound image to the community.

We are dedicated to employing local people, fostering independence, and making positive contributions to the district and province's economy through the application of the best business principles and guidelines. Our strong understanding of service delivery and personalized attention allows us to ensure that we deliver not just great but exceptional service.
